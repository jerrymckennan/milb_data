This is a simple set up that has links to show additional data for some of the Tiger minor league affiliates. Specifically, there are links for the Toledo Mud Hens and Lakeland Flying Tigers, both of which have Statcast data that we view.

All of the data comes from Savant and gets broken down into a few different displays. The data is updated weekly on Monday and will be constantly a work in progress.

Should you stumble upon this and enjoy it, please consider subscribing and following Tigers ML Report on YouTube, Patreon, or any social media apps of your preference.
