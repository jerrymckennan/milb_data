Lakeland BIP data

BIP Note: GB have launch angle < 10 degrees, Low-LD have launch angle between 10 and 17 degrees, High-LD have launch angle between 17 and 25 degrees, FB have launch angle between 25 and 50 degrees, and PU are > 50 degrees.

Lakeland hit metrics

Hit notes: Hard Hit is EV > 95, Sweet Spot is between 8 and 32 degrees, Barrels are EV > 95 hit in the air and have a combined BA >= .500 and SLG >= 1.500

Lakeland spray data

Spray Note: < -30 degree is LF, between -30 and -15 is left-center, between -15 and 0 is center-left, between 0 and 15 is center-right, between 15 and 30 is right-center, > 30 is RF.
